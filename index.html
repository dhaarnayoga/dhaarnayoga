About Us

We have been practicing yoga, meditation and allied therapies since 10 years and We always thrive to promote and strengthen our vedic culture and yoga sutras in it's purest form, because it is not only our belief but a hard fact that if we want to establish India as a world leader then it is not possible without connecting to our roots, our rich heritage and vedic culture. Following the ideology behind our name Dhaarna, we want to inspire and give direction to thought process of society which believes that yoga is not for everyone. We in Dhaarna want to establish yoga as a part of life style. Our scientific approach and refined techniques are designed to reproduce the belief of people in yoga as a life style.
